Our People

Head Chef - Owner

I am the orchestrator of culinary symphonies in the kitchen. With years of expertise and a passion for the art of cooking, I've mastered the delicate balance of flavors and the precision of techniques. My kitchen is a stage where fresh ingredients, innovation, and creativity come together to create unforgettable dining experiences. I thrive on pushing the boundaries of taste and presentation, aiming to delight the palate and nourish the soul with every dish that leaves my domain.

Sous-Chef

I'm a passionate sous chef with a deep love for the culinary arts. In the heart of the kitchen, I've honed my skills and knowledge, working alongside talented chefs and exploring a myriad of flavors and techniques. Whether it's perfecting a classic dish or experimenting with innovative culinary creations, my goal is to bring a burst of taste and a touch of finesse to every plate. Together, we'll embark on a flavorful adventure, where food is not just a meal but an exquisite experience.

Junior Chef

I'm an aspiring junior chef, fueled by a deep love for all things culinary. In the bustling kitchen, I'm like a sponge, soaking up knowledge and experience as I assist and learn from seasoned chefs. While I'm still sharpening my skills and perfecting my craft, I approach every task with enthusiasm and dedication. My culinary journey is just beginning, and I'm excited to explore new flavors, techniques, and creations.

Purchasing Manager

I'm your dedicated purchasing manager, adept at navigating the intricate web of supply chains and procurement. With a keen eye for quality and a knack for sourcing the best deals, I'm here to ensure your organization's supply needs are met efficiently and cost-effectively. From negotiating with suppliers to optimizing inventory levels, my goal is to streamline operations and maximize value. Whether it's sourcing raw materials, managing vendor relationships, or staying on top of market trends, I'm your go-to resource for all things procurement.

Barista

I'm your friendly neighborhood barista, your go-to coffee connoisseur, and the artist behind your daily caffeine fix. With a deep passion for the perfect brew, I've honed my craft to whip up espresso-based masterpieces that'll awaken your senses. From latte art to brewing techniques, I'm here to make your coffee experience exceptional, one cup at a time. Whether you prefer a classic cappuccino or a trendy cold brew, I'm your dedicated bean whisperer, ready to craft your favorite coffee with a smile.

Administrator

I'm the dedicated administrator at your service, the backbone of efficient operations and seamless organization. With a keen eye for detail and a knack for problem-solving, I ensure that everything runs smoothly behind the scenes. From managing schedules and resources to handling paperwork and overseeing logistics, I'm here to keep things in order and make your life easier. Whether it's coordinating meetings, optimizing workflows, or tackling the nitty-gritty administrative tasks, you can count on me to provide essential support, allowing you to focus on what you do best.
